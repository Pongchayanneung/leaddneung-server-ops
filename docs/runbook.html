③ What the alerts mean

"leaddneung alive" at 08:00 & 20:00 — healthy heartbeat. Normal.

"X auto-recovered" — a service crashed and the watchdog restarted it. No action needed; just FYI.

"X DOWN — restart FAILED" / disk / RAM / GPU temp — needs you. See §④.

No heartbeat at 08:00 or 20:00 = the server or your home network is dead (likely power/internet outage at home). Nothing you can do remotely; check when back or ask someone home to power-cycle.
